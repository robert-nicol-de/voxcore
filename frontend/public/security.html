How VoxCore Protects Your Data

1️⃣

Query Inspection

Every AI-generated query is intercepted and analyzed by the firewall before reaching the database.

2️⃣

Multi-Layer Validation

Syntax check, semantic analysis, policy matching, and pattern recognition all run in parallel.

3️⃣

Risk Scoring

Each query receives a risk score based on multiple factors including injection risk, data sensitivity, and policy violations.

4️⃣

Decision Making

Queries above risk thresholds are blocked. Safe queries proceed. High-risk queries are flagged for review.

5️⃣

Audit & Alert

Every decision is logged with full context. Real-time alerts notify admins of blocked or risky queries.

6️⃣

Compliance Reporting

Export audit logs, generate compliance reports, and demonstrate your security posture to regulators.
