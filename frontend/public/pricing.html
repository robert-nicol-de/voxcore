Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free trial? ▼

The free trial includes full access to VoxCore in preview mode. You can explore the UI, test the SQL firewall, and see how query governance works. Preview mode disables actual database connections.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime? ▼

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing? ▼

Yes. All prices shown are for annual billing and include a 20% discount compared to monthly plans.

What databases are supported? ▼

VoxCore currently supports Snowflake, SQL Server, and PostgreSQL. BigQuery, Redshift, and other databases are coming soon.

Is there a monthly billing option? ▼

Yes. Monthly billing is available at a 20% premium. Contact sales for details or upgrade from your dashboard.

Do you offer team features? ▼

Team features and SSO integration are available on the Enterprise plan. Contact our sales team to learn more.
